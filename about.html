About Me

Photography has been a lifelong passion of mine and was a key part of my Fine Art degree, where I regularly used photography as inspiration for my work.

Alongside my creative background, I spent seven years working within the property industry, giving me a strong understanding of how professional photography can help showcase a property and attract prospective buyers or tenants.

Today I am pursuing property photography professionally, combining an artistic eye with industry knowledge to create bright, natural images that present properties at their best.

Based in Hertfordshire and available across Hertfordshire, Essex and London.
